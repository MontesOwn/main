Welcome to Your Dream Vacation Home

A Magical Stay Awaits

Our beautiful vacation home, just a short drive from Disney, ensures a relaxing and magical stay. Nestled in Orange Tree, one of Central Florida’s most prestigious communities, our home is perfectly placed along Highway 27, just 2 miles north of US 192. The community is only 9 miles from Disney World and within easy reach of all major theme parks and attractions.

Property Highlights

Orange Tree offers its own 5-acre manicured grounds, featuring a children’s playground, tennis court, pickleball courts, soccer pitch, and basketball court, all surrounded by fragrant orange groves. Additionally, the community is in close proximity to Lake Louisa State Park, a beautiful and unique area with rolling hills and scenic landscapes where visitors can swim, hike, bird watch, picnic, fish, or kayak/canoe.

  • Prime Location: Just a short drive to Disney and Universal attractions
  • Private Pool: Enjoy your own private oasis
  • Outdoor Oasis: Dine and lounge in your private outdoor area
  • Spacious Accommodation: Ideal for families and groups
  • Stay Connected: Complimentary high-speed WiFi
  • Modern Kitchen: Well-equipped with modern stainless appliances
