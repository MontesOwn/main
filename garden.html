Our Garden’s Bounty

Our garden is a diverse and colorful space, filled with a variety of flowers and vegetables. Here are some of the highlights:

  • Tomatoes: We grow four different types of tomatoes, each with its own unique flavor and texture. These tomatoes are the star ingredients in our homemade pizza and spaghetti sauces.
  • Poblanos: These mild chili peppers add a delicious kick to our dishes. We enjoy using them fresh or roasted in a variety of recipes.
  • Cucumbers: Crisp and refreshing, our cucumbers are perfect for salads, pickling, and snacking.
  • Pumpkins: Our pumpkins are not only great for fall decorations but also for making delicious pies, soups, and roasted seeds.
  • Okra: This versatile vegetable is a staple in our garden, used in everything from gumbo to grilled dishes.
  • Brussels Sprouts: These nutrient-packed veggies are a favorite in our household, especially when roasted to perfection.

Flowers

In addition to our vegetables, we also cultivate a variety of flowers that add beauty and attract beneficial pollinators to our garden. The vibrant blooms create a picturesque and inviting environment.

Homemade Delights

One of the most rewarding aspects of our garden is the ability to create homemade culinary delights. Our tomatoes are transformed into rich and flavorful pizza and spaghetti sauces, enjoyed by family and friends alike. The fresh produce from our garden enhances our meals, providing a farm-to-table experience that we cherish.

Sustainable Practices

We are committed to sustainable gardening practices that promote the health of our plants and the environment. In addition to straw bale gardening, we use organic fertilizers, practice crop rotation, and employ natural pest control methods to maintain a healthy and productive garden.

Join Our Gardening Journey

We invite you to explore our gardening practices and discover the joys of growing your own food. Whether you’re an experienced gardener or just starting out, we hope our journey inspires you to embrace sustainable gardening and enjoy the fruits of your labor.
